EVENTIFY WORKS IN FOUR SIMPLE STEPS
1- SEARCH

It is simple. On the main page enter the event you are looking for. Alternatively, you can search for event type and related results will be listed.

2- LOCATE

After searching, you need to select your event. You can locate your event and get a address description as well. Locate your event and go to next procedure

3- APPLY

When you locate your event, it is time for buying the ticket. You can follow the steps and you can pay for credit card or money order. After payment, there is a qr code special for your transaction.

4- ATTEND

This is the last and most entertaining part. Just go to the event and show the qr code, so you can enter the event.
